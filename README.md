# ERCO Power BI Performance Dashboard

## Overview

The ERCO Power BI Performance Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution designed to provide a comprehensive view of organizational performance through intuitive visualizations and actionable insights. The dashboard consolidates operational data into a centralized reporting platform, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify trends, and make informed business decisions.

### Key Features

* 📈 Executive KPI dashboard for high-level performance monitoring
* 📊 Driver and segmentation analysis to identify performance patterns
* 🏢 Entity-level deep dive for detailed operational insights
* 🔄 Funnel and process performance tracking
* ⚙️ Operational efficiency and productivity metrics
* ⚠️ Risk, quality, and exception monitoring
* 🎛️ Interactive filters and drill-down capabilities for dynamic analysis

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Summary

Provides an overview of critical business metrics, performance trends, and strategic KPIs.

### Drivers & Segmentation

Analyzes the primary factors influencing performance across different segments and categories.

### Entity Deep Dive

Enables detailed analysis of individual entities to compare performance and identify improvement opportunities.

### Funnel / Process View

Visualizes process flow and conversion stages to highlight bottlenecks and process efficiency.

### Operational & Efficiency Metrics

Tracks operational performance over time using trend analysis and productivity indicators.

### Risk, Quality & Exceptions

Monitors operational risks, quality issues, and exceptions requiring management attention.
